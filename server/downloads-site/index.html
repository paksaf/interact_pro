iOS

iPhone & iPad · iOS 15.5 or later

Download IPA

iOS doesn't allow direct APK-style installs. You sign the IPA with your own free Apple ID on a Mac or PC, then install on your iPhone over USB. Two options:

  1. Sideloadly (Mac & Windows, easiest) — sideloadly.io. Drag the IPA in, plug in your iPhone, sign in with your Apple ID. Re-open Sideloadly every 7 days to refresh the signature.
  2. AltStore (Mac, automatic 7-day re-signs while on the same Wi-Fi) — altstore.io.

On first launch the iPhone will say "Untrusted Developer". Open Settings → General → VPN & Device Management and tap Trust for your Apple ID.

Android TV / Fire TV

Sony Bravia · Mi TV · Sharp Aquos · Fire TV Stick / Cube · Google TV

Download APK

Same APK as Android phone. Sideload onto your TV in two steps:

  1. On the TV: Settings → Device Preferences → Security & restrictions → Unknown sources — enable for the app you'll use to push the file.
  2. Install "Send Files to TV" on phone and TV (free, both Play Stores). On the phone, open the app, pick the downloaded InteractPro.apk, tap your TV.

PDF reader, library, OCR view, and book-flip work fine with the remote. Touch-only features (signature, scanner) need a phone or a paired Bluetooth touchpad.
