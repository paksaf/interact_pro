How to install on Android
  1. Tap Download APK above. Your browser may warn that APKs aren't from Play Store — that's expected.
  2. Open the downloaded InteractPro.apk from your Notifications shade or Files → Downloads.
  3. If prompted, allow your browser/file manager to install unknown apps (one-time setting).
  4. Tap Install. The app icon appears in your launcher.
  5. Verify integrity (optional): shasum -a 256 InteractPro.apk should match the SHA-256 above.
